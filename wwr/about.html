White Water Rafting

Founded in 1995, Dry Oar Rafting began as a small family business with a passion for outdoor adventures. Our founders, John and Sarah Rivers, started with just two rafts and a dream to share the thrill of whitewater with the world.

History

Today, we're proud to be one of the top-rated rafting companies in the region, with over 50 expert guides and a commitment to safety and environmental stewardship. Every year, we introduce thousands of adventurers to the magic of river rafting.

All people are welcome to this adventure! Don't waste your time and come with us to have a fun time!
